Welcome to Forecasting

This app keeps you updated with daily weather forecasting details for selected locations.

How to use?

1. Add a bookmark

  • Go to home screen and click on add button at bottom right corner.
  • Please give location permission to get your current location.
  • Click anywhere on map to select location.
  • Now click on done button at bottom right corner.
  • The added location will show on home screen city list.

2. Check weather

  • Go to home screen and select city.
  • It will navigate to weather details screen. Here you can see all details

3. Remove city from bookmark list

  • Go to home screen and click on delete icon of city which you want to delete.
  • Then confirm to delete.
